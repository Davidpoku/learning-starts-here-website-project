E-Books

Ebook; the format of the academic future, our books use multi-media. You can read our content, see and listen users can also talk to us about ideas, and respond on our blog. Our e-books automatically fit the scale and format of any users' mobile device. Embedding audio and video within text makes our e-books more interactive. Users can also personalise their learning experience by changing the fonts and font sizes to suit their needs..

Games

Games as an educational tool provides opportunities for deeper learning. We may think we're pretty smart, but in fact we have very little notion of how humans learn. Kids know: They play games. Until, that is, they go to school. That's when the games stop. And often, so does the learning.We believe games are architectures for engagement

Video

When we were students, ‘using video in education’ mostly meant showing a movie in class. Which to the team at Design space 1 solutions , as students, was clearly much better than the alternative of a lecture. With so much interactive multimedia technology available to today’s students, ‘video in education’ means way more than it used to.
